### **1.2 WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?**

* **Automatically collected information:** Some information — such as your IP address and device characteristics — is collected automatically via third-party SDKs. This is primarily needed to maintain the security and operation of our Services and for advertising purposes. Additionally, we receive anonymized crash reports and app statistics from Google Play to help us troubleshoot issues and improve the stability of the app.
* **In-app purchases & payment data:** The application offers in-app purchases. All transactions are fully handled and stored via the **Google Play Billing** system. We do not process or store any of your financial information (such as credit card numbers).

---

### **1.3 HOW DO WE PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION? (Legal Basis)**
We process your personal information based on the following legal grounds according to the GDPR:

* **Consent (Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR):** For personalized advertising via **Google AdMob**. You grant this consent via the in-game consent banner (UMP).
* **Contractual Necessity (Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR):** To facilitate and manage in-app purchases made through the Google Play Billing system.
* **Legitimate Interests (Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR):** To keep our Services safe, secure, and technically functional.

---

### **1.4 WHEN AND WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE YOUR DATA?**
We share data with the following third-party providers. Please review their respective privacy policies:

* **Ad Networks:** [Google AdMob & Ads Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* **Payment Processors:** [Google Play Billing](https://payments.google.com/payments/apis-aup)

### **1.5 DO WE COLLECT INFORMATION FROM MINORS?**
We do not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under **13 years of age (or 16 years of age in the EU/EEA)**. If we learn that personal information from users less than the required age has been collected, we will deactivate the account and take reasonable measures to promptly delete such data.

---

### **1.6 YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS**
Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. You can withdraw your consent for personalized advertising at any time within the app's settings. For any requests, contact us at **rotti.dev@gmail.com**.
