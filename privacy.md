### **1.2 WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?**

* **Automatically collected information:** Information such as your IP address, device characteristics, and the Android Advertising ID (AAID) is collected automatically via third-party SDKs. This is required for the security, technical operation, and the provision of personalized advertisements. We also receive basic, anonymized crash reports via the standard Google Play Console to improve app stability.
* **In-app purchases & payment data:** The application offers in-app purchases. All transactions are fully handled and stored via the **Google Play Billing** system. We do not process or store any of your financial information (e.g., credit card numbers).

---

### **1.3 HOW DO WE PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION? (Legal Basis & Retention)**
We process your personal information based on the GDPR:

* **Consent (Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR):** For personalized advertising via **Google AdMob**. You grant this consent via the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) within the app.
* **Contractual Necessity (Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR):** To facilitate in-app purchases made through Google Play.
* **Legitimate Interests (Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR):** To keep our Services safe and functional. We do not use automated decision-making or profiling.

**Retention:** We only retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

---

### **1.4 DATA TRANSFERS & THIRD PARTIES**
We share data with the following providers. Google may act as an independent data controller for certain activities. Data may be transferred to countries outside the EU/EEA (e.g., the United States). We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure an adequate level of data protection.

* **Ad Networks:** [Google AdMob & Ads Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* **Payment Processors:** [Google Play Billing](https://payments.google.com/payments/apis-aup) *(Currently inactive / being prepared for future updates)*

---

### **1.5 DO WE COLLECT INFORMATION FROM MINORS?**
We do not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under **13 years of age (or 16 years of age in the EU/EEA)**. If we learn that personal information from underage users has been collected without parental consent, we will promptly delete such data.

---

### **1.6 YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS**
Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, restrict the processing of, or erase your personal data, as well as the right to data portability. 

**Opt-Out:** You can withdraw your consent for personalized advertising at any time within the app's settings or by resetting your device's Advertising ID in your Android settings. 

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. For any requests, please contact us at **rotti.dev@gmail.com**.
